Teens' Decline

Instagram has struggled with retainning and engaging teenagers. Against this background, I designed & launched a global survey with Instagram new users to size top barriers for teens to join Instagram.

I played a key role in orchestrating the entire project, including deployment of the survey, data processing & analyses using SQL and Rstudio and socialization of the findings. To complement the survey insights, I collaborated with a qualitative researcher to conduct follow-up interviews with select participants.

Challenges

A few challenges emerged during communications of key findings to stakeholders. For example:

  • How to interpret the relative role & importance of parent gatekeeping and lack of reason (to use Instagram), both of which were top reported barriers from teens' responses to the survey?
  • What does having nothing to share on Instagram, the top driver for feeling no reason to use Instagram, really implicate?

Impact

The findings has driven a few net new product initiatives and marketing campaings that aimed to encourage teens' willingness to share authentically on Instagram.

Video Search

Intentional video consumption, primarily from video search, is critical for effective delivery of in-stream video ads. Hence Facebook wanted to optimize frequency and successes of video search on the platform. In response, I initiated & conducted a diary study comparing search experiences on Facebook and YouTube in two distinct markets, the US and Mexico.

Despite not being part of the search team, I assumed a leading role throughout this study, including managing research vendors, conducting data analyses, reporting and socialization of the findings.

Research questions

There are a few major research questions to answer in this study:

  • What caused the huge gap between Facebook and Youtube in video search? Specifically, ranking or inventory?
  • Despite the same UI/UX, why did Facebook video search perform much better in Mexico than in the US?

Impact

This study provided not only tactical suggestions for UI/UX of video search in the short term (e.g. parity between WWW and mobile devices), but also strategic recommendations for the team to work towards in the long term (e.g. trending search, acquisition of how-to content).
